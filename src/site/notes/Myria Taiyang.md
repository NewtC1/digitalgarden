#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- Created when a flash of light struck East Ocean's sun, Taiyang. The sun afterwards emitted an ice-blue lumen light. It then descended from the heavens, creating the [[East Ocean\|East Ocean]] empire.
- This is pretty much the only information we have on this religion.
- Taiyang was once worshipped in [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]], but the religion declined after [[East Ocean\|East Ocean]] surrendered during the war.
- It's believed that Taiyang was a fragment of [[Myria Helius\|Myria Helius]] that entered the local sun.
