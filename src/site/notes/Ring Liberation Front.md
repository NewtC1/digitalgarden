#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- [[Bones\|Bones]] is a prominent member.
- One of the many factions against [[United Mining\|United Mining]] in the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]].
- This faction formed during the middle of the war, and briefly held two [[Lumen Caves\|lumen cave]]s. From here they struck at various [[United Mining\|United Mining]] strongholds, even claiming a farming station. Unfortunately they were forced to abandon the caves when the [[Myrian Witching Tower\|Myrian Witching Tower]] joined the war, as the witches made it impossible to use them as bases. They were nearly wiped out by the United Mining [[Witch\|Witch]] fleet.
- Lives on as [[Bones Brigade\|Bones Brigade]].
- Motto: "Endless Lumen, light the way, that the belt may rise again one day."
