Myrian god of the never ending flow of space time. Represents the connection across time and space regardless of distance.
- Some people believe there are two Binarii gates in [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]] which pass through space and time. Entering the gates will show whatever they desire.
- Symbol of reincarnation
- Relatively few followers compared to other gods
	- Still more than [[Excidium\|Excidium]].
- The subspace rift at Shiratsuka is considered to be sacred to Binarii.
	- It's implied the subspace rift is connected to the East Ocean transport station
- Binarii guards the gates of space and time.
