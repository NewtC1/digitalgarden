#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- Solar system [[Echo of Starsong\|Echo of Starsong]] takes place in.
- Location of the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]].
- Dominant religion [[Myria Terra\|Myria Terra]], [[Myria Ignis\|Myria Ignis]] and [[Myria Helius\|Myria Helius]].
- Central star is the same [[Myria Ignis\|Ignis]] from the Myria Terra myths.
- Home to numerous [[Lumen Caves\|Lumen Caves]], all once belonging to the ancient [[Myrian Civilization\|Myrian Civilization]].
- Space burials are banned in Thousand Peaks by [[United Mining\|United Mining]]
- Most Peakers are not friendly to either [[Witch\|Witch]]es or [[East Ocean\|East Ocean]]ers due to their influence in the war.
- Once, there were supposed to be ten planets, called [[The Ten Peaks\|The Ten Peaks]]. Now there are only four.
	- Four peaks of Heaven:
		- West Mount Tonitrus
		- East Mount Immanis
		- South Mount Aurora
		- North Mount Malefica
	- Six peaks of Earth
		- East Mount Fons, slain by Malefica
		- North Mount Cantus, slain by Malefica
		- South Mount Avis, slain by Tonitrus
		- West Mount Aqua, slain by Aurora
		- Excidium Ultimus, slain by Immanis
		- Excidium Primus, slain by Immanis
- On the very outskirts of Thousand Peaks is the black hole [[Excidium\|Excidium]].
