### Summary
A pair of survivors of an apocalyptic disease work together to hold a [[Space Burial\|Space Burial]]. [[John Mason\|John Mason]], constantly besieged by the [[ghosts\|ghosts]] of those who passed before him, scavenges for parts. [[Fei Lin\|Fei Lin]], the last remaining [[Earthology\|Earthology]] [[Witch\|Witch]], works on the rockets needed for the burial.
