- Nebulous material that is used to fuel spaceships
- Multidimensional in nature
- Lumen is referred to differently depending on the state of matter it's in
	- Liquid - Lumenide
	- Gas - Lumium
- Can allow memories to connect across space and time.
- Influenced by song
- Mined from [[Lumen Caves\|Lumen Caves]]
- The material that was fought over in the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]]
- [[United Mining\|United Mining]] is in the process of refining lumen, which seems to remove the "consciousness" from it. The resulting lumen can no longer be sensed by witches but is a far more efficient fuel source.
- Lumen sensitivity can lead to being immersed in the memories of lumen, leading to seeing events from the past or maybe even future.
- When [[Jun Lee\|Jun]] gets lost in the flow of memories, he can see some of the events from the [[Myrian Civilization\|Myrian Civilization]].
- Lumen seems to be conscious in some way, though it's hard to say if it's specific parts of lumen or the entirety.
- Extremely stable and not effected by heat in any way. However, sound can cause it to change states. The sound of this process is called a [[Starsong\|Starsong]].
