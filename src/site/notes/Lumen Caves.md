#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- Asteroid that function as mines for [[Lumen\|lumen]].
- Also seem to be ancient [[Myrian Civilization\|Myrian Civilization]] shrines.
- Caves can be mined to exhaustion, causing their [[Starsong\|Starsong]] to stop broadcasting.
- A dying mine has a noticeably different [[Starsong\|Starsong]] than a newly discovered one.
- Many of the [[United Mining\|United Mining]] caves are barren, leaving nothing behind but an asteroid hulk.
- Caves that have been converted have a lot of extra equipment moved into mine the [[Lumen\|Lumen]]
- Caves are owned by different families and factions.
