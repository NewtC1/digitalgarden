#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- [[Edalune Grant\|Eda]], [[Red Grant\|Red Grant]], and [[Remi Dorian\|Remi]]'s ship. Later also becomes home to [[Jun Lee\|Jun]].
- Old Lumen Seeking Vessel from the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]].
- Serial is QBC-OPUS-03.
- Has a bio-amplification scanner to enhance [[Edalune Grant\|Eda]]'s damaged voice.
- The crew worked a seller of flowers primarily. Because of this, it has an extensive greenhouse.
- Many of its functions from the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]] have been revoked, limiting its capabilities.
- Red Chamber's motto on the side says "Serve none but yourself. Embrace yourself with all your flaws, and listen to the voice within."
