#theme #universal 

One of the most prevalent universal themes. Frequently paired with [[Memory\|Memory]]. 
## Questions
- What forms an identity?
- What makes us happy or sad with our identity?
- What causes our identity to change?
- Is identity required for happiness?
- Who am I?
- Who do I want to be?

## Example pairings
- I am my memories.
- I am my relationships.
- I choose who I want to be.
