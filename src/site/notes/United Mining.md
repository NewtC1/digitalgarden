#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- Winner of the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]] and occupying force in [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]]
- Seat of power is the city of [[Ironwind\|Ironwind]].
- After the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]] it briefly held full control over all [[Lumen Caves\|Lumen Caves]], but due to continued fighting after the war ended it made [[Lumen\|Lumen]] a publicly available resource. This meant that explorers could claim mining rights as long as they were able to sample a perfect [[Starsong\|Starsong]] from a new cave
- Out of fear of the rising influence of the [[Myrian Witching Tower\|Myrian Witching Tower]], they denounced the witches for their part in the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]] and refused to pay them for their help. This shattered faith in the Witching Tower.
- Rules [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]] from [[Ironwind\|Ironwind]].
- Motto: "May fire and iron light the stars."
- Uses a process call "consciousness extraction" to purify [[Lumen\|lumen]] further.
