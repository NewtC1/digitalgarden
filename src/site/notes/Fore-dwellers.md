#tainted_grail 
Fore-dwellers are the are the four-armed natives of Avalon. They were mostly wiped out in the war with [[King Arthur\|King Arthur]] and his forces, making them very rare in the [[Tainted Grail\|Tainted Grail]] games. They were capable mages, and made use of the [[Wyrdness\|Wyrdness]] present in the mists of [[Avalon\|Avalon]] to produce near miraculous effects.

# Known Fore-dwellers
## False All-mother
This Fore-dweller was summoned by the priestesses of the main [[All-mother\|All-mother]] shrine. While she isn't the All-mother, she was forced into that role. This corrupted the shrine and led to her being trapped in the area until the player comes by to release her and cleanse the corruption.

If the player hunts down the secret areas, she is also revealed to be the origin of the Red Death, which was unleashed by her grief when she lost her son, [[Fore-dwellers#The Prince\|The Prince]]. She didn't do this intentionally, and deeply regrets the damage this did on the human realm.
## Weaver
The Fore-dweller in charge of weaving reality on [[Avalon\|Avalon]], hence the name. He is used by the [[Fore-dwellers#False All-mother\|False All-mother]] as a way to solve the twin problems of the corrupted [[Wyrdness\|Wyrdness]] and her missing son at the same time.
## The Prince
The Fore-dweller that was merged with [[King Arthur\|King Arthur]] during [[Merlin\|Merlin]]'s experiments with captive Fore-dwellers.
## The Prisoners
A pair of Fore-dweller prisoners, these two are the ones who gave the Dal Riata the ability to craft [[Wyrdstone\|Wyrdstone]]. They reached out to humanity in good faith, but were instead chained as prisoners of war. The Dal Riata then used them as the basis to found the [[Stonewarden\|Stonewarden]]s.
## Unnamed Wyrdstalker
This knight appears from the mists during a [[Wyrdnight\|Wyrdnight]], hunting the player down. They don't speak, instead just attacking immediately. They are extremely powerful and mostly serve as a way to force the player to use a [[Wyrdfire\|Wyrdfire]] until they're strong enough to fight and kill them. This might be more than one Wyrdstalker, as he respawns after certain story points are reached.
