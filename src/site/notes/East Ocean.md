#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
- One of the main participants in the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]].
	- Participated as an ally to [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]], but were forced to abandon the war early.
- Home of [[Jun Lee\|Jun]] and [[Kay Volans\|Kay]].
- Main religion is [[Myria Taiyang\|Myria Taiyang]].
- May be a stand-in for China? Seems to be directly related to Chinese culture. All of their space stations have a Chinese influence in their design.
- Very far away from [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]].
- Ruled by an emperor.
- Run by numerous noble families. Families are numbered.
- Rife with political machinations.
- One of the families sends an assassin after [[Jun Lee\|Jun]].
- The land is infertile, so they rely on [[Lumen\|lumen]] to prosper.
- Uses a "kowtow" ritual as a sign of respect.
- Clans
	- Lee
		- Clan that tried to negotiate with [[United Mining\|United Mining]] during the [[Lumen War\|Lumen War]]. Failed negotiations led to East Ocean abandoning the war early.
		- [[Jun Lee\|Jun]]'s clan.
		- Jun is  the heir to this clan.
