#tainted_grail 
Pure potential that infests the mists of [[Avalon\|Avalon]]. This force was used by the [[Fore-dwellers\|Fore-dwellers]] as magic, and is heavily influenced by emotions. The human invaders of [[King Arthur\|King Arthur]] attempted to use the Wyrdness, but unlike the Fore-dwellers they didn't have control over their own emotions. This led to the Wyrdness being twisted and corrupted.
