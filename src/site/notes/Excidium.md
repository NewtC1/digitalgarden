#EchoOfStarsong #OPUS 
Excidium is the black hole deity that represents the netherworld. His realm is the hell of the Myrian religion, and where evildoers go when they die.

	- Desacrators 
- The least common religion of the [[Thousand Peaks\|Thousand Peaks]].
