#theme #universal 
Heroes generally refer to individuals in a society who go above and beyond what they're called to do for the general good. This shows up a lot in superhero media, but can be more understated.
### Questions
- What defines a hero?
- Does a hero need to do universal good?
- What happens to a person after the heroic deed?
- How do others see a hero?
- What drives someone to take a heroic action?
