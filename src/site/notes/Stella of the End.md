#stellaoftheend 
Stella of the End is a visual novel published by [[Visual Arts/Key\|Visual Arts/Key]]. The game follows the journey of [[Philia\|Philia]] and [[Jude\|Jude]] as they travel to meet a mysterious benefactor. The game has numerous themes including the human relationship with technology, definition of love, [[Humanity\|Humanity]], and AI.
