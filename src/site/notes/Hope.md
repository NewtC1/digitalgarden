#theme #universal 
A common, possibly the most common, theme. Hope is frequently paired with [[Desire\|Desire]], [[Despair\|Despair]] and [[Dreams\|Dreams]]. Hope is in many cases the driving force in stories, with it being the reason why characters do most of their actions, at least when paired with dreams. It's a very simple universal.

The [[Principle of Contrasts\|Principle of Contrasts]] - Hope shines brighter as it shrinks. A good story will emphasize the dying of hope without taking away the gravity of its existence.
## Questions
- What do you hope for?
- How do you keep hope?
- What makes you lose hope?
