Goddess of music and wielder of [[Lumen\|Lumen]] energy. First god to receive the gift of song from [[Myria Helius\|Helius]]. Her voice channeled the flow of [[Lumen\|lumen]]. Wherever she went, flowers bloomed.

Terra was later killed by Six Peaks of Earth when [[Myria Ignis\|Ignis]] bellowed against the actions of [[Myria Helius\|Helius]]. Her body would be interred in [[Excidium\|Excidium]], in the hope that she and [[Myria Helius\|Helius]] would one day meet again.
